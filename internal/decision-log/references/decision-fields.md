# Decision Fields

## Recommended Entry Shape

1. Decision number
2. Title
3. What we decided
4. Why
5. Consequences
6. Status

## Writing Guidance

- Keep titles short and scannable.
- State the decision in concrete language.
- Explain why in a few bullets or a short paragraph.
- Include consequences only when they materially affect later work.
- Mark whether the decision is active, superseded, or under review when relevant.

## Good Candidates

- "Snapshot is the first layer of the full report"
- "AI does not own scoring"
- "Use additive migration instead of rewrite"

## Bad Candidates

- "Renamed helper function"
- "Changed button padding"
- "Updated linter config"
